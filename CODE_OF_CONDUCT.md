# Code of conduct

## Our standard

We want participation in Carry to be safe, respectful, and constructive.
Contributors must treat others with empathy, accept technical disagreement in
good faith, respect privacy, and focus criticism on the work rather than the
person.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate outing,
publishing another person's private information, and sustained disruption are
not acceptable in project spaces or when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when conduct is harmful. Decisions should consider
context, impact, prior behavior, and the safety of those affected.

To report conduct privately, contact a maintainer through the repository's
private security-reporting form and mark the report as a code-of-conduct matter.
If that form is unavailable, open an issue requesting a private contact channel
without including personal or sensitive details. Reports will be handled as
confidentially as practical; conflicts of interest should be disclosed and
recused.
